About Charito Murphy

Charito Murphy has always had a passion for innovation . From her time as a high school student she always excel at her class and graduated as Valedictorian. Believing she could contribute an innovation in medical industry she decided to study Bachelor of Science in Pharmacy and graduated with awards at the University of Bohol Philippines.

She decided to migrate in Australia to further enhance her knowledge and education. At the onset of her stay in Australia and while travelling back and forth overseas , the building construction amazed her and wondered if she could innovate a concrete walls that can be a life changer for the building industry/ She decided to develop an interior and exterior decorative concrete walls piled and installed into one.

Amidst the criticisms , lack of support from family and friends, ridiculed and mocked and criticised she continued to develop the idea in helping the construction industry, bring more employment, and help ordinary people to have a taste of classy, safe, strong, and affordable home to live.

In almost 5 years years of innovation , Charito has chosen not to give up. Because of her strong determination, believing that women in construction plays a vital part for the economy, she invented the first ever interior and exterior decorative concrete walls. She managed to construct houses and apartments in Philippines using the decorative concrete blocks as the area of start-ups and giving employment to a small group of people and eventually to have the team around the globe.
